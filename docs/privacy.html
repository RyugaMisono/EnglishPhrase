Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

English Phrase Bot ("the App") is a personal automation tool operated by a single individual developer. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not:

  • Collect, store, or process personal data of any third party
  • Share data with any third parties
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Maintain user accounts or profiles

3. API Credentials

The App stores only the developer's own API credentials (OAuth tokens) for accessing social media platforms. These credentials are stored securely as encrypted environment variables and are never shared.

4. Content Data

The App generates and stores educational content (English phrases, example sentences) on the developer's own infrastructure. No user-generated content or third-party personal data is involved.

5. Third-Party Platforms

The App publishes content to social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, X). Each platform's own privacy policy governs how published content and associated data are handled.
